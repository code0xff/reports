No publication-blocking evidence gaps remain.

## Resolved during gather

- Primary Stripe/Tempo/MPP sources collected: Stripe MPP announcement, Stripe machine payments docs, MPP site, Tempo homepage/docs/GitHub, and paymentauth.org Internet-Draft specifications.

## Non-blocking evidence limits

- Public MPP transaction volume, failure rates, fraud/dispute rates, and merchant ROI are not yet available.
- Quicknode labels its MPP integration alpha; implementation evidence should be treated as early signal.
- The arXiv helper script returned HTTP 429 during collection. The same arXiv papers were available through web fetch and are cited.
- Mastercard and IMF URLs returned 403 to curl but were readable through web fetch; this is treated as bot/access blocking rather than broken links.
- MPP is changing quickly; session, streaming, payment method, and discovery details may evolve.
