# Uncertainties

- MPP launched recently; public transaction volume and production reliability metrics are not available.
- MPP may still be changing quickly, including session, streaming, payment method, and discovery primitives.
- Tempo appears to be a default or privileged stablecoin rail, not the exclusive settlement layer; multi-chain and non-Stripe processor adoption should be treated as emerging.
- Visa/card support, Stripe SPT support, and Stellar support show broadening beyond Tempo stablecoins, but adoption by non-Stripe processors remains uncertain.
- Public commentary is vendor-heavy because the protocol is new.
- The largest unresolved operational question is how agents will enforce budgets, avoid spending loops, and recover from prompt-injection or context-binding failures in production.
